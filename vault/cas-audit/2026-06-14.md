   Eigenständigkeit *vollständig gemäss Aufgabenstellung*. Until the owner replaces the
   paraphrase with the standard FFHS wording and signs (name/place/date/signature), the tier
   is capped at 7. Everything else in this chapter already meets the 12-pt evidence bar, so
   this 5-point swing is gated solely on the signature. **Owner action — cannot be delegated
   (it is itself Veto 3).**

   floor confirms layer/module structure; the prose claims "documentation at non-trivial
   places" without a quoted docstring/excerpt in the report. One short code excerpt or a
   pointer to representative docstrings would close the last point to 7.
